## Hero

## What This Project Is

AIXYZ shows the public layer of an AI-assisted web operations studio: offers,
demos, intake flows, receptionist concepts, and project receipts.

## Why It Matters

Small businesses need practical AI systems that can be understood before they
are bought. AIXYZ makes the offer visible while keeping private workflows,
client records, credentials, and operational infrastructure protected.

## How It Works

Visitors reach AIXYZ through the public site, review offers and demos, submit
intake, and move into owner-reviewed workflows. Private operations and receipts
stay protected.

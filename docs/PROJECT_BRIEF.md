# Project Brief

## Project

AIXYZ

## Purpose

AIXYZ is a public AI web studio surface for demonstrating practical small
business AI websites, offer pages, receptionist workflows, and operational
receipts.

## Public Value

The project lets buyers and collaborators understand the offer without exposing
the private engine. It shows:

- Public-safe business demos.
- AI receptionist and intake concepts.
- Fiverr launch surfaces.
- WordPress-powered public routes.
- Protected operations and receipt discipline.

## Audience

- Small business owners.
- Marketplace buyers.
- Web/AI collaborators.
- Public reviewers of Faith Cheltenham's AI studio work.

## Status

Public surface prepared. Private engine retained outside this repository.
